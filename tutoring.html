While earning a Masters Degree in Educational Psychology and a Bachelors Degree in Secondary Education in Mathematics, I have learned and used many different online learning tools. I use a combination of the best (free) online resources that allow students to become directly involved in content developed by myself, their teachers, and other high quality resources.

Students are able to observe examples in real time, collaboratively interact with an online white board to complete problems with 1-on-1 guidance, and save their own copy of the whiteboard to study from.
