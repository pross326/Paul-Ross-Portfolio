About

I taught high school mathematics for 6 years, where I developed high level skills in management, organization, and communication. As an inner city school teacher, I learned the importance of decision making, perseverance, and patience. After teaching an Algebra-based computer programming course, I began to take a deeper interest in coding and taught myself web development online. I transitioned from a math teacher interested in coding, to a web developer interested in math. I am a lifelong learner, I love being challenged, and I always have a goal in mind!
